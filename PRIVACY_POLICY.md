# Privacy Policy for EyeRest - Protect Vision

**Last Updated:** April 15, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect
The **EyeRest - Protect Vision** Chrome Extension operates securely and entirely on your local machine.
- We **do not** collect, transmit, or sell any personal data or usage metrics.
- All your settings (break intervals, durations, custom messages) are stored locally in your browser using `chrome.storage.local`.

## 2. Permissions
The extension requests the following permissions to function correctly:
- **`alarms`**: Used to schedule your break reminders in the background with zero battery drainage.
- **`storage`**: Used exclusively to save and retrieve your customized preferences locally.
- **`notifications`**: Used to push standard desktop alerts when it's time to rest your eyes.
- **`tabs`**: Used to conditionally open the interactive 20-20-20 exercises page.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. Your data never leaves your browser space.

## 4. Updates to this Policy
We may periodically update this policy if new Chrome features require different background permissions. Any changes will be documented transparently in our Web Store release notes.

## 5. Contact
For any questions regarding your privacy or the extension's behavior, please reach out via the Support tab on the Chrome Web Store.
